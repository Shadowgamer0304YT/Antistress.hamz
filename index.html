About Bubble Pop

Bubble Pop is a mindfulness-focused anti-stress game created to help you find moments of calm in your busy day. In a world full of overwhelming content and constant notifications, we believe in the power of simple, beautiful experiences.

This game is designed by someone who understands the importance of mental health and the need for digital tools that actually help rather than harm our well-being.
